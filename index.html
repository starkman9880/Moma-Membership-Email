How to see and do more at MoMA
Dive into membership
Ready to see, do, and experience more at MoMA? You’ve unlocked a suite of membership benefits, giving you exclusive access, engaging programming, special discounts, and more. Here’s the rundown:
Get unlimited access

There’s always something new to discover in our ever-evolving galleries. And with $5 guest passes, you can share every visit with friends and family.
Save on good design

Enjoy 10% off your purchase at MoMA Design Store, free shipping, and extra savings during Member Shopping Days.
See it first and last

Get a sneak peek at new exhibitions during Member Previews, and enjoy a final glimpse at Last Looks.
Connect from a distance

Experience MoMA online with digital events like Evening In and more member-only content.
